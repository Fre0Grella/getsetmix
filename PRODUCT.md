# Product

## Register

product

## Users

DJ/producers managing a personal Rekordbox library on a laptop, usually in a home studio or on the go, who need to stage clean batches quickly before downloading.

## Product Purpose

Ingest tracks from URLs into Rekordbox with minimal manual cleanup. Success looks like a clean staging list ready to download, with metadata confirmed and no surprises during ingestion.

## Brand Personality

Minimal, premium, precise.

## References

MediaHuman, Notion

## Anti-references

Complicated, baroque, overly stylized UIs. Salesy product-marketing patterns or anything that feels like a pricey upsell surface.

## Design Principles

- Make the staging list the hub, everything else supports it.
- Reduce decision points, keep the flow linear from URL to batch.
- Surface status clearly, avoid decorative noise.
- Prefer reversible actions and explicit confirmations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal target beyond good contrast. Respect reduced motion preferences and avoid relying on color alone for status.
